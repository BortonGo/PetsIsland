# Third-Party Notices

Pet Island includes selected pixel-art animation frames from
[tonybaloney/vscode-pets](https://github.com/tonybaloney/vscode-pets), pinned
at commit `d661785e890c422999bdec739dcc0a6b65d6f1cd`.

The imported animals are limited to:

- fox (`media/fox/red_*`), artwork by Elthen;
- parrot (`media/cockatiel/gray_*`), artwork by Marc Duiker;
- bear (`media/panda/black_*`), artwork by Jessie Ferris;
- lizard (`media/deno/green_*`), artwork by the vscode-pets contributors.

No cat assets are included. The vscode-pets media notice states that the cat
artist asked that those assets not be freely distributed through GitHub.
The German shepherd frames are original Pet Island assets and do not use the
`vscode-pets` dog animation.
