# Privacy Policy for Pet Island

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Pet Island is a free and open-source application. It does not create user accounts, display advertising, include analytics SDKs, or track users.

## Data stored on the device

Pet Island stores the selected pet, pet name, app settings, active session and aggregate session history locally in the app's private container. This data is used only to provide the application's features. It is not transmitted to the developer or any third party.

## Live Activities

When a session starts, iOS displays the selected pet and session timing information through ActivityKit on the Lock Screen and, on supported devices, in Dynamic Island. This information remains on the device and is managed by iOS.

## Network access and third parties

Version 1.0 doesn't require an account or server and doesn't send personal data over the network. The app doesn't include third-party analytics, advertising or tracking libraries.

## Data removal

Ending a session removes its active state. Deleting Pet Island from the device removes the app's locally stored data according to iOS behavior.
